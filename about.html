About Dawn Outdoor Products

Leading OEM Manufacturer of Golf Cart Solutions

Our Story

Dawn Outdoor Products (Shuguang Outdoor Products) is a leading manufacturer of premium golf cart rain enclosures and weather protection solutions. With years of experience in the industry, we've established ourselves as a trusted partner for golf resorts, hotels, dealers, rental companies, and communities across the United States, Canada, and worldwide.

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer service has made us the preferred choice for OEM and custom manufacturing projects in the golf cart accessories market.

Our Mission & Values

To provide the highest quality golf cart rain enclosures and weather protection solutions through innovative manufacturing, exceptional customer service, and factory-direct pricing.

Quality
